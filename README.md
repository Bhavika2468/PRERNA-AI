# PRERNA AI — AI Career Compass for Rural Girls

PRERNA AI is an AI-powered multilingual career guidance platform designed to empower rural students especially girls by providing personalized, accessible, and intelligent career support.

Many students in rural communities lack access to mentors, career counseling, scholarship awareness, and skill development opportunities. PRERNA AI bridges this gap using Artificial Intelligence, voice technology, and personalized learning pathways to help students discover careers, build confidence, and achieve their goals.

# PROBLEM

In rural India, career guidance is still a luxury.
Most students only know about a limited number of careers.

They often lack:
* Access to mentors
* Personalized career counseling
* Scholarship awareness
* Skill development guidance
* Interview preparation
* Emotional support and confidence building

As a result:
* Students choose careers blindly
* Talented girls drop out early
* Opportunities remain inaccessible
* Potential gets wasted

The biggest issue?
The internet has information.
But rural students need direction.

That gap inspired us to build PRERNA AI.

# SOLUTION

PRERNA AI is an intelligent multilingual career guidance platform specifically designed for rural students.

It combines:
* AI powered career recommendations
* Voice based interaction
* Skill gap analysis
* Scholarship discovery
* Mentor matching
* Emotional confidence analysis

The goal is simple:
To convert confusion into clarity.
Instead of giving generic advice, PRERNA AI creates personalized career pathways based on:
* Student interests
* Learning level
* Confidence level
* Career aspirations
* Available opportunities

And the best part?
The entire experience is designed to work in local languages with voice first interaction.
