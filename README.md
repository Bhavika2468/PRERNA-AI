# PRERNA AI — AI Career Compass for Rural Girls

PRERNA AI is an AI-powered multilingual career guidance platform designed to empower rural students — especially girls — by providing personalized, accessible, and intelligent career support.

Many students in rural communities lack access to mentors, career counseling, scholarship awareness, and skill development opportunities. PRERNA AI bridges this gap using Artificial Intelligence, voice technology, and personalized learning pathways to help students discover careers, build confidence, and achieve their goals.

# Problem We Solve

Millions of rural girls in India face limited access to:

* Career guidance
* Mentorship
* Industry exposure
* Skill development resources
* Financial opportunities

As a result, many talented students remain unaware of their true potential and career possibilities.

PRERNA AI aims to make career guidance accessible, inclusive, and personalized for every student regardless of geography or background.

# Features

* 🎙️ Voice-first interaction for low-literacy users
* 🌐 Multilingual AI chatbot support
* 📊 AI-powered skill gap and learning analysis
* 🛣️ Personalized 3–6 month career roadmaps
* 🎓 Scholarship and opportunity recommendations
* 👩‍🏫 Mentor matching system
* 🧠 AI mock interview simulator with feedback
* 💬 Emotional support and confidence analysis
* 📈 Student analytics and progress tracking dashboards

# Tech Stack

* Frontend: React.js / HTML / CSS / Tailwind
* Backend: Node.js / Express
* AI & NLP: OpenAI APIs / NLP models
* Database: Firebase / MongoDB
* Voice Processing: Speech-to-Text APIs
* Data Visualization: Dashboards & Analytics Tools
